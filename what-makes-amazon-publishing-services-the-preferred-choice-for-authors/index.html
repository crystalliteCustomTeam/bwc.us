What Do You Need to Provide to Amazon to Publish Your Book or Other Content?

Before you can publish your book or other content on Amazon, there are a few things you need to provide. First, you'll need to create an account with Amazon. How Much Does It Cost to Use Amazon Publishing Services, And How Do You Pay for Them?

Amazon offers various publishing and book editing services that authors can use to self-publish their books. These services include eBook formatting, cover design, and editing. Authors can choose to use as many or as few of these services as they like, and they only pay for the services they use.

eBook formatting starts at $99, and cover design starts at $149. Editing prices vary depending on the length of the book and the level of editing required, but authors can expect to pay between $0.002 and $0.004 per word. Amazon also charges a 40% commission on eBooks sold through its platform.

So, for example, if an author sells an eBook for $2.99, Amazon will keep $1.20, and the author will receive $1.79. Overall, the cost of using Amazon book editing services is relatively low, especially compared to traditional publishing houses. And because authors only pay for their services, they have complete control over their budget.

The Marauder and His Daughter

Linda S. Cunningham’s "The Marauder and His Daughter" became a #1 Best Seller in the Biographies of World War II category on Amazon. Published on platforms like Amazon, Goodreads, and AbeBooks, we provided comprehensive marketing support to ensure its success and visibility.

  • Delivered extensive marketing services, including social media management, SEO, and targeted ads.
  • Created and monetized a YouTube account and produced a book video trailer.
  • Organized PR activities including a live Q&A session and ads in major publications like the NY Times and Houston Chronicles.

The Devil & Details

Dave F. Woody's "The Devil & Details" secured the #2 spot in Biography & Memoir Short Reads on Amazon. Published exclusively on Amazon, our targeted marketing efforts ensured high visibility and reader engagement.

  • Launched a focused Amazon Ad Campaign for optimal visibility.
  • Designed a dedicated website to showcase the book.
  • Enhanced discoverability with comprehensive SEO and active social media marketing.

The Incredible Candus

Lawrence Birnbaum’s “The Incredible Candus” became a beloved favorite, earning the #3 spot on Amazon. Published on Amazon, Barnes & Noble, eBay, Google Books, and Goodreads, we implemented diverse marketing strategies to engage readers and boost sales.

  • Launched a user-friendly website to showcase the book and the author.
  • Published book trailer on Times Square.
  • Launched a targeted Amazon Ad Campaign to maximize reach.
  • Enhanced visibility through book reviews and consistent newsletters.

A Craniosacral Story

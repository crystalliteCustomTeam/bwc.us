What is The Scope of Book Writing in 2022?

The scope of book writing today is much different than it was centuries ago. For one, there are many more opportunities for authors to get their work published. With the advent of self-publishing and online publishing platforms, anyone with a story to tell can share their work with the world. This has led to an increase in the diversity of books being published, and has made it easier for new authors to get their start.

Another change in the book writing landscape is the rise of e-books and audiobooks. E-books can be read on devices like smartphones and tablets, while audiobooks can be listened to on phones, cars, and other listening devices. This has made books more accessible to people who may not have the time or patience to sit down and read a physical book.

Finally, social media has had a major impact on the scope of book writing. Authors can now interact directly with their readers, and build up a following of people who are interested in their work. This allows authors to reach a wider audience than ever before, and connect with readers in new and exciting ways.

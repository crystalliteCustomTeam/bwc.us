The Coach With ‘IN’
– A Book Publishing & Book Marketing
Case Study

Get A Free Quote
866-600-0036

Author’s Objective:

The process started when renowned author Bob Teseny—who has impressive corporate sales training and a basketball background—came to us with his novel, The Coach With ‘IN.’ His goal was to provide coaches from all walks of life with the knowledge and skills he had acquired throughout his spectacular career. Given this book's potential, we were excited to take on the challenge of making it more widely available to readers through our book publishing & book marketing strategies.

Book Overview:

Coach Teseny's The Coach With ‘IN’ is an inspiring book for aspiring sports coaches. Teseny provides insightful advice on coaching, leadership, and personal development based on his experience coaching basketball champions and in corporate sales. Prepare for a fun and educational experience to help you become a better leader and coach.

The Challenge

Our initial challenge that we encountered was to publish the book on Amazon. Since the book was only sports-related, we executed a specialized approach to reach the right audience. So, the book needed to gain well-deserved attention from the right audience. Our book publishing services team looked after this matter to secure satisfactory visibility on Amazon KDP. After publishing, our goal was to build a buzz around the book that would help in marketing the book.

Book Publishing Strategies Implemented by Book Writing Cube

Now that the author had Book Writing Cube as his publishing partner, it was up to us to make sure The Coach With ‘IN’ would live up to the greatest expectations. Our book publishing service included thorough editing, accurate formatting, and a creative cover design to create a book that would make a big impression on digital platforms and bookshelves and effectively communicate Coach Teseny's message.

Book Editing & Formatting

We reviewed the draft for grammatical and spelling mistakes to ensure the writing is clear, concise, and flows smoothly. Furthermore, we carefully proofread the whole manuscript, looked at the formatting according to Amazon’s standards, and created a visually appealing layout for both paperback and digital formats so that it could achieve remarkable results.

Book Cover Design

After analyzing the book's original cover, our team came to a conclusion that it fell short of capturing the core of the book, we then discussed this matter with the author and showed him the cover design we made for his book. The cover was redone to represent the core concepts of the book better – a motivational image capturing the reader's attention. The author loved our design and instantly approved it to replace the old book cover.

The Final Call: Marketing The Book

Our book marketing company then strategized and came up with a plan of utilizing various social media platforms for advertising the book and then we optimized the book listing in a way that it appears to readers at the top. We started working on this and after a month we got to see some amazing results. The book sold out on Amazon within a month because of our persistent effort and comprehensive marketing plan.

Outcomes & Results

With The Coach With ‘IN,’ our devoted team achieved remarkable success after weeks of relentless involvement in publishing and marketing. With a flurry of positive reader reviews, it declared itself as Amazon's most popular book, achieving the author's long-held aspiration.

Wrapping Up

If you are an author looking to publish and market your book on Amazon KDP then, Book Writing Cube is the right place to achieve the kind of success just like coach Bob Teseny did with his book on Amazon.

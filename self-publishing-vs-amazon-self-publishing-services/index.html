Comparing The Costs: Self Publishing vs. Hiring Amazon Self Publishing Services

By BWC Team in 2023

Amazon self publishing services have become increasingly popular among aspiring authors in recent years. The idea of publishing a book without the need for a traditional publishing house is appealing to many, and Amazon has made it easier than ever before. However, as with any service, there are costs associated with self book publishing like any other publishing method that every author must know.

While the upfront cost of using Amazon self publishing service may seem minimal, there are other factors to consider, such as editing, formatting, and cover design, which can all impact the book's overall quality. Additionally, self publishing authors are responsible for all marketing and promotional efforts, which can be time-consuming and expensive.

On the other hand, professional self publishing services offer a team of experts who can guide authors through the publishing process, from editing to marketing. While the cost may be higher, the result is often a higher-quality product with greater visibility and potential for success.

In this blog post, we will explore the costs of self book publishing versus professional self publishing services and help authors decide which option is best for them. So, let's dive in!


Taking Control of The Publishing Journey: Self Publishing on Amazon

Self publishing has revolutionized the world of literature, offering authors a platform to publish their work without the need for traditional publishing houses. Amazon KDP Publishing is a leading platform for self-publishing, offering a range of benefits that make it an attractive option for authors. Here we will explore the advantages of self publishing on Amazon and some of the costs that authors should be aware of.


Advantages Of Self Publishing

The following are some of the advantages of self-publishing on Amazon:



  • Ease of Use

One of the main advantages of self publishing on Amazon is the ease of use. The platform offers a simple, user-friendly interface that allows authors to upload their work quickly and easily. The process is streamlined and intuitive, making it accessible to authors of all skill levels.


  • Control Over The Publishing Process

Self publishing on Amazon offers authors complete control over the publishing process. It means that authors can make all the decisions regarding their book's content, cover design, formatting, and marketing. They can choose their release date, set their own pricing, and make changes to their book anytime. This level of control is often not possible with traditional publishing houses.


  • Higher Royalties

Another advantage of self publishing on Amazon is the higher royalties that authors can earn. Amazon offers authors a royalty rate of up to 70%, significantly higher than traditional publishing houses' royalties. It means that authors can earn more money from their book sales, which can be especially beneficial for self-published authors who rely on their writing as their primary source of income.


The Costs Of Self Publishing On Amazon

The following points describe the factors that are taken into consideration when budgeting for self publishing on Amazon:


  1. Editing And Proofreading

    While self publishing on Amazon offers many benefits, there are also some costs that authors should be aware of. One of the most important costs to consider is editing and proofreading. Authors who self-publish are responsible for ensuring their work is well-written and error-free. It may require the services of a professional editor or proofreader, which can be a significant expense.

  2. Cover Design And Formatting

    Another factor which increases the cost of self publishing on Amazon is cover design and formatting. Authors who self publish are responsible for creating an eye-catching cover that will attract potential readers. They must also ensure their book is formatted correctly for digital and print publication. While some authors may have the skills to do this, many will need to hire a professional designer, which can be another significant expense.

  3. Marketing And Promotion

    Finally, marketing and promotion can be also a factor when discussing the cost of self publishing on Kindle Direct Publishing. While the platform offers a range of marketing tools and resources, authors still need to invest time and effort into promoting their book to ensure it reaches their target audience. It may include creating a website, building a social media presence, and running advertising campaigns. These activities can be time-consuming and costly, but they are essential for attracting readers and generating sales.

Comparison Of The Total Costs Of Self-Publishing Vs. Amazon Self Publishing Services

Amazon publishing allows authors to publish their books directly without the help of a professional publishing service. Authors can use Amazon's self-publishing tools to format their manuscripts, design their book covers, and upload their books to the Amazon store. However, this option requires significant effort and time, as authors have to handle everything themselves, from editing to marketing.

On the other hand, hiring an Amazon self publishing service offers authors a wide range of services, including editing, formatting, cover design, and marketing. These services come at a cost, but they can help authors save time and ensure their books are high quality.


Which Option Is More Cost-Effective?

When it comes to the total costs of self-publishing versus Amazon self publishing services, the answer is not straightforward. Self-publishing on Amazon is free; authors only pay when their books are sold. Amazon takes a percentage of each sale, ranging from 35% to 70%, depending on the book's price, format, and other factors. However, authors must bear the costs of editing, cover design, formatting, and marketing.

On the other hand, professional self publishing services offer various packages with different prices depending on the services included. For example, a basic package may include editing, formatting, and cover design, while a premium package may include marketing and promotional services. These packages can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars.

So, which option is more cost-effective? It depends on the author's budget, goals, and level of expertise. If an author has the necessary skills and resources to handle the editing, cover design, formatting, and marketing themselves, self-publishing on Amazon may be more cost-effective. However, if an author wants professional help and support to ensure their book is of high quality and reaches a broader audience, a professional self publishing service such as Book Writing Cube is the ideal choice in the long run.

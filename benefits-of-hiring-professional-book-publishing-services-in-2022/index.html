How Much Do Book Editors Cost

?

According to marketplace data from 2022, hiring an editor for a 50,000-word book costs between $2,500 and $3,500. Book editing services cost is determined by various factors, including the editor's level of experience, the service type, and the project's length and genre. A copy editor may charge $0.021 per word, whereas a proofreader may charge $0.020 per word. How much authors spend on book editors depends on how much authors make selling their books.

How to Self-Publish a Book

?

You're not alone if you've ever wondered what steps to take when self-publishing a book. The first step in amazon self-publishing your book is choosing a platform. Several platforms offer various services. However, not all of them are created equal. It is important to understand which services are important to you and which ones are not. Also, you need to know the differences between self-publishing service platforms.

Before choosing a platform, it is crucial to understand your book's goals and then consider each option's pros and cons. This way, you can choose the right option for your book.

ISBNs are very important to self-publishing. An ISBN, or International Standard Book Number, is a unique identifier for a book. It will help bookstores and libraries locate it. In addition to the ISBN, a barcode will help distributors find your book.
